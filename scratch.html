How to Get Up on a Wakeboard

  1. Start in the Water
    • Float on your back with knees bent
    • Keep the board perpendicular to the rope
    • Let the board float naturally under you
  2. Let the Boat Do the Work
    • Don’t pull with your arms — stay relaxed
    • Let the boat’s pull bring you up
    • Stay crouched with knees close to your chest
  3. Stand Up Smoothly
    • Once on the surface, slowly extend your legs
    • Shift your weight slightly to your back foot
    • Keep the handle low and near your lead hip

Getting up is just the beginning — here's what you're working toward!
